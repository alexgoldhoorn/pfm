    Methodology
    Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM)

    Each asset class is projected independently using a simplified GBM model. The mean path is deterministic compound interest; the confidence bands scale with time:

    mean(t)  = S₀ × (1 + r)^t
upper(t) = mean(t) × e^(+z × σ × √t)
lower(t) = mean(t) × e^(−z × σ × √t)
    AssetVolatility σ
    Stocks / ETFs16%
    Bonds6%
    Cash1%
    Mortgage Amortization

    The mortgage is reduced month-by-month using standard amortization:

    Monthly interest  = balance × (annual_rate / 12)
Principal payment = monthly_payment − monthly_interest

    Net Worth = total liquid assets (cash + stocks + bonds) − remaining mortgage balance at each year.

    Data & sources

    Prices, fundamentals and news come from Yahoo Finance (refreshed daily at 20:00 UTC; fundamentals/news are cached briefly).

    Cost basis, gains and tax use your own transactions (FIFO). Tax is a Spanish IRPF savings-base estimate, not advice.

    LLM/AI answers are generated from your data + cited news — informational, not financial advice.

    What's New

    Recent improvements to Portfolio Manager.

    • Net Worth New page tracking your whole picture — investments (auto) plus off-brokerage cash, property, pension, mortgage and loans → total net worth. FIRE goals now project from this total, not just the brokerage.
    • Tax Per-lot FIFO tax report with CSV export, and a year-end tax optimizer (harvestable losses, estimated tax saved, Spain's 2-month wash-sale flag). P2P/savings interest is now a first-class transaction type counted in the IRPF savings base.
    • Research Workbench with autocomplete, your position (cost basis, P/L, sell calculator, average-cost chart), Yahoo fundamentals with sources, LLM analysis, and a downloadable Markdown report per ticker.
    • AI Chat The assistant now reads your real portfolio (holdings, performance, history), renders Markdown, and works in dark mode.
    • Analytics Split into lazy-loaded tabs (Performance & Net Worth · Dividends · Gain/Loss · Tax · Risk & Diversification · Fees); a gain/loss leaderboard, dividend forward income & calendar, per-holding concentration, European benchmarks, and clearer tooltips and source labels.
    • Performance yfinance results cached (sector/country, fundamentals, benchmark, news), slow lookups moved off the event loop, and per-transaction DB lookups removed — pages load much faster. Plus a dashboard alerts banner (price targets & watchlist buy zones).
    • Data New "Index fund" asset type; cash deposits/withdrawals shown inline in Transactions; dashboard shows positions per type.
    • UI Real dark mode across the app, grouped sidebar navigation, and this Help / What's New section.
